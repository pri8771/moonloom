## What Is Moonloom?

Moonloom: Idle Dream Factory is a mobile idle game for iPhone. In an idle game, your factory keeps producing even when you're not playing. You check in, collect your earnings, upgrade your factory, and watch it grow.

In Moonloom, you run a magical factory that collects whispers from sleeping towns, spins them into dreamthread, weaves them into dreams, and ships those dreams to the moon to restore its light.

**One sentence:** Build a dream factory, restore the moon, prestige and do it all over again — but bigger.

---

## The Story

The moon has gone dark. The sleeping world has lost its dreams. As the keeper of an ancient Moonloom, you are the only one who can rebuild the Dream Factory and restore moonlight to the world.

Each dream your moth couriers deliver to the moon slowly restores its glow. When the moon is fully restored... a New Moon Reset allows you to start over, stronger than before.

## How The Game Works

### The Basic Loop (What You Do Every Session)

1. **Open the app** — see how much your factory produced while you were away
2. **Collect earnings** — tap to collect all the dreamthread, moonlight, etc.
3. **Buy upgrades** — spend your earnings to make buildings produce faster
4. **Buy more buildings** — each building type produces different resources
5. **Watch the moon glow** — as you produce dreams and moonlight, the moon restores
6. **Prestige** — when the moon is restored, reset for permanent bonuses

### What You're Building (The Factory)

Your factory has 12+ types of buildings, each producing something the next one needs:

- **Whisper Nets** catch whispers from sleeping towns
- **Lullaby Wells** amplify the whispers
- **Dreamthread Spindles** spin whispers into dreamthread
- **Memory Looms** weave dreamthread into dream fabric
- **Nightmare Filters** purify the dreams
- **Star Dye Vats** add starlight to the dreams
- **Moth Courier Nests** send moths to deliver dreams to the moon
- **Cloud Packaging Line** packages dreams for delivery
- **Dream Atlas** maps the delivery routes
- **Comet Shipping Dock** adds express delivery
- **Lucid Observatory** amplifies moonlight
- **Moonheart Engine** powers the moon restoration itself

### The Currencies

| Currency | What It Is | How You Get It |
|----------|-----------|----------------|
| Whispers | Basic resource | Whisper Nets + sleeping towns |
| Dreamthread | Mid-tier resource | Dreamthread Spindles |
| Moonlight | Progression currency | Moth Couriers delivering dreams |
| Stardust | Premium soft currency | Login bonuses, events, small purchases |
| Lucid Shards | Prestige currency | Earned when you reset (New Moon Reset) |

### Prestige: The New Moon Reset

When you restore enough of the moon, you can trigger a **New Moon Reset**. This:
- Resets your factory back to the beginning
- Gives you **Lucid Shards** based on how much you restored
- Lucid Shards unlock permanent upgrades in the **Lunar Codex**
- Each reset is faster because of your permanent upgrades
- The game gets deeper and more rewarding with each reset

This is the "prestige" mechanic — the reason idle games stay fun forever.
